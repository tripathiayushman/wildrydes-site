How Does This Work?

In today’s fast paced world, you’ve got places you need to be but not enough time in your jam packed schedule. Wouldn’t it be nice if there were a transportation service that changed the way you get around daily? Introducing Wild Rydes, an innovative transportation service that helps people get to their destination faster and hassle-free. Getting started is as easy as tapping a button in our app.

Download The App

Head over to the app store and download the Wild Rydes app. You’re just a few taps away from summoning a mythological taxi with… questionable visibility.

Request The Gray Sisters Taxi

Need to get somewhere fast (or at least loudly)? Request a ryde in the app and we’ll dispatch the Grey Sisters Taxi immediately. Yelling about who gets to hold the Eye included. Navigation accuracy not guaranteed.

Pick A Price

Choose how many drachmas you’re willing to part with. No surge pricing — the only surge is the sudden, unexpected acceleration when the Sisters start arguing.

Ride Off To Destiny!

Once you accept their terms (No screaming, no grabbing the Eye, no questioning the route) the Grey Sisters Taxi will arrive in a blur of sparks and shouting. Hold tight. Your destination is… probably where you’ll end up.

Our Story

WildRydes began when two mortal tech workers — a software developer and a financial analyst — accidentally stumbled through a tear in the Mist during a failed meditation retreat. Instead of inner peace, they discovered the Gray Sisters: three ancient beings who share one magical eye and an old, extremely aggressive taxi. The Sisters complained endlessly about “modern mortals never calling anymore,” and the founders realized something: They weren’t retired… just unemployed. Moved by a mix of terror, sympathy, and questionable entrepreneurial instinct, the founders created an app to connect desperate riders with immortal drivers who sort of know where they’re going. Using their combined expertise in coding and convincing mythical beings to sign contracts, WildRydes was born. Today, the Gray Sisters Taxi serves demigods and mortals across multiple realms — loudly, quickly, and occasionally to the wrong destination.
